Mason Protter (Jan 24 2022 at 19:46):

This is known as trait based dispatch.

using ScientificTypes

f(x) = f(scitype(x), x)

f(::Type{Continuous}, x) = x + 1
f(::Type{Count}, x) = x - 1

julia> f(1.0)
2.0

julia> f(1)
0
