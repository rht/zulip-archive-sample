Advent of Code 2021 is starting soon. If you want to participate or follow along, check out #advent-of-code (2021) and #advent-of-code-spoilers (2021). :holiday_tree:

You can discuss and share your solutions, tricks and tips with other participants there. The stream is not exclusive to Julia, plenty of people participate in other languages as well. A popular Julia community leaderboard can be joined using 213962-a8828aa0 as invite code.
