Mason Protter (Oct 02 2020 at 15:20):

Hey everyone, this is a regular reminder that we want this Zulip to be a welcoming, civil and constructive environment.
Any violations of the Julia Community Standards which you agreed to by joining this workspace will be taken seriously by the Zulip admins.

If you see or experience any violation of those standards, or feel unsafe or upset, please contact us by sending a private message to the @zulip-admins user group.

You can also contact the Julia stewards by emailing stewards@julialang.org, in particular if your concern or conflict involves the admins and you would prefer a third party to resolve it.
